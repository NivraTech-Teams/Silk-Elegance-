Our Story

Discover the journey of Silk Elegance

Weaving Dreams Since 2010

Silk Elegance was born from a passion for traditional Indian textiles and a vision to make them accessible to the modern woman. Founded by sisters Priya and Meera Sharma, our journey began in a small workshop in Mumbai with just three weavers.

Today, we collaborate with over 200 artisans across India, preserving ancient weaving techniques while infusing contemporary designs. Each saree in our collection tells a story - of skilled hands, rich heritage, and timeless beauty.
